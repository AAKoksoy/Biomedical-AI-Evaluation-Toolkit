# FOCUS Lab — Week 01

## Research Question

**What does it mean to evaluate an AI system?**

## Learn

Concepts studied this week:

* evaluation objectives
* intended use
* evaluation datasets
* ground truth
* metrics
* failure analysis
* limitations

## Build

A small hands-on evaluation exercise will be added during the week.

## Reflect

Questions guiding this week's reflection:

* What makes an AI system good enough for its intended purpose?
* Who decides what counts as a correct answer?
* Can a model perform well on a benchmark but still be unsafe?
* How should biomedical risk change the evaluation process?
* What information is hidden by aggregate performance metrics?

## Original Idea

Biomedical AI evaluation should measure more than technical performance. It should also examine clinical relevance, uncertainty, severity of errors, population-specific risks, human oversight, and the consequences of model failure.
