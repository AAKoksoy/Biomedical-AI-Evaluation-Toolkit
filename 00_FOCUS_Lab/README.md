# FOCUS Lab

FOCUS Lab is the working research journal of the Biomedical AI Evaluation Toolkit.

Its purpose is to document the development of practical and rigorous approaches to:

* biomedical AI evaluation
* benchmark design
* validation frameworks
* healthcare AI governance
* safety testing and red teaming
* medical reasoning evaluation
* human-AI quality assurance

Each week follows four activities:

1. **Learn** — study one evaluation concept.
2. **Build** — implement a small evaluation exercise.
3. **Reflect** — examine assumptions, limitations, and failure modes.
4. **Publish** — share one polished insight through GitHub and LinkedIn.

Experimental work begins here. Mature and reusable outputs are later organized into the relevant evaluation modules.
